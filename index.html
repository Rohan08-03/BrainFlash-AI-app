Privacy Policy for BrainFlash AI

Effective Date: October 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

BrainFlash AI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

Locally Stored Data (On Your Device Only):

  • Your notes (title, content, and category)
  • Generated flashcards
  • Daily AI usage counter
  • Note edit history timestamps

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

When you export notes, files are saved to your device storage and can be shared via your chosen apps. Exported files remain under your control.

AI Processing Data:

When you generate flashcards, your note content is temporarily sent to our AI service provider (Groq AI) to generate study questions. This data is:

  • Used only for flashcard generation
  • Not stored by us or the AI provider after processing
  • Transmitted securely via HTTPS

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking
  • Device identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

Your notes remain on your device. Features include:

Flashcard Generation:

  • Note content is sent to Groq AI for processing
  • AI generates study questions
  • Results are returned and stored locally on your device
  • No data is retained on servers

Note Export:

  • You can export notes as text files to your device
  • Files are saved locally and shared via apps you choose
  • We do not track or access exported files

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties except:

  • Groq AI - Only when you actively request flashcard generation for that specific note

4. Rate Limiting

To prevent abuse of the AI service, our backend temporarily stores your IP address for 24 hours to enforce the 5 flashcard generations per day limit. This data:

  • Is stored only in server memory (not in a database)
  • Resets automatically after 24 hours
  • Is not linked to your identity
  • Is not used for tracking or analytics

5. Data Security

  • All data stored locally using secure device storage (AsyncStorage)
  • API communications encrypted with HTTPS
  • Backend uses rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • No cloud backups or syncing
  • You control your data - delete anytime via Settings
